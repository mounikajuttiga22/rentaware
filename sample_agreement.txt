# RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement ("Lease") is made on this 1st day of March, 2026, by and between:

**Landlord**: Mr. John Doe, 123 Property St., City.
**Tenant**: Mrs. Jane Smith, 456 Current Ave, City.

### 1. PROPERTY
The Landlord agrees to lease to the Tenant the residential property located at:
Apartment 101, Sky High Towers, Tech Park Road, City.

### 2. LEASE TERM
The term of this lease shall be for 11 months, commencing on March 1, 2026, and ending on January 31, 2027.

### 3. RENT
The Tenant agrees to pay a monthly rent of $1,500. Rent is due on the 1st of each month. A **late fee** of 10% shall be applied if rent is not paid within 5 days of the due date.

### 4. SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Tenant shall pay a **security deposit** of $9,000 (equivalent to 6 months of rent). This deposit is refundable at the end of the lease, subject to deductions for damages.

### 5. NOTICE PERIOD
Either party must provide a **notice period** of 4 months before terminating this lease.

### 6. LOCK-IN PERIOD
The Tenant is subject to a **lock-in period** of 12 months. Early termination during this period will result in forfeiture of the security deposit.

### 7. RENEWAL
A **renewal** option is available if both parties agree 30 days before the lease expiry.

### 8. MAINTENANCE
The Tenant is responsible for monthly **maintenance charges** of $100.
